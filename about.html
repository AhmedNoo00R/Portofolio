About

Career-driven at first.
A real passion now.

My path into cybersecurity started out career-oriented — I needed a direction, and security looked like a solid one — but somewhere along the way it turned into a genuine passion.

I'm a Computer Science student at Khwaja Fareed University of Engineering & Information Technology (KFUEIT), Rahim Yar Khan, Pakistan — currently in my 7th semester, with 3+ years of self-study in cybersecurity running alongside my degree.

I recently completed an 8-week SOC Analyst internship at Axeronix Technologies, where I worked hands-on with Wazuh SIEM, Sysmon endpoint logging, and Windows Event Viewer to detect and investigate security events. Alongside this blue-team foundation, I have practical, self-taught experience in offensive security through manual exploitation on PortSwigger Academy and Hack The Box.

I'm now working toward eJPTv2, building toward my long-term goal of penetration testing, web application security, and red team operations — with OSCP+ as the target certification.
